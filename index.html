Data Visualization Options for the Navigator Research Website.

Below are a few solutions for visualizing data. Option One is custom, while Option Two is supported by Datawrapper.

Option One: Custom

While still in the prototype phase, Option One showcases the potential styling that will align with Navigator Research's future brand and web aesthetic. Fonts, colors, borders, spacing, and corner radius are independently customizable, allowing for precise alignment with brand specifications

However, the greatest advantage of this approach is its ability to offer full customization without the need for expensive third-party tools. Visualizations will render responsively on any device. This method provides a practical and adaptable framework, enabling tailored branding and establishing a solid foundation for future development and enhancements.

Option Two: Datawrapper

This option leans on Datawrapper, a tool that creates interactive charts and data visualizations. The free version allows you to easily build multiple charts, however you cannot modify colors or remove the Datawrapper branding on the free plan.

The Custom plan enables you to tailor the appearance and remove the Datawrapper branding which is priced at $599/month. Regardless of plan, the charts are captivating and will provide a strong fundamental infrastructure for publications and reporting.

Conclusion

In conclusion, each solution is robust, offering unique benefits. The custom approach provides complete flexibility and brand precision without recurring costs, though it requires a larger upfront developmenteffort. In contrast, Datawrapper offers an easy-to-use platform for interactive charts, but full customization and watermark removal come at $599/month. Rest assured, either choice will provide a robust foundation for allowing you to create engaging and informative data visualizations.
